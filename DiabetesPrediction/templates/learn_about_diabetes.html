3. Best Foods for Diabetes

  • Leafy greens (Spinach, Kale)
  • Whole grains (Brown rice, Quinoa)
  • Fatty fish (Salmon, Mackerel)
  • Legumes (Beans, Lentils)
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Nuts and Seeds
  • Avocado
  • Berries (Blueberries, Strawberries)
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Olive oil

4. How to Maintain a Healthy Lifestyle

  • Eat a balanced diet with whole foods.
  • Exercise regularly (walking, cycling, yoga).
  • Monitor blood sugar levels frequently.
  • Drink plenty of water and avoid sugary drinks.
  • Get enough sleep (7-9 hours per night).
  • Manage stress through meditation and relaxation.
  • Regularly consult with a doctor.
